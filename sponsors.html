Why Sponsor Hackswift?

Sponsors at HackSwift can send mentors, showcase products, and promote their brands, gaining access to the potential of the next generation of programmers and making a lasting impression on our diverse audience, many of whom are first-time coders. This platform offers a unique opportunity to connect with young coders, expand your influence, and potentially find skilled summer interns, as the hackathon performance serves as a strong indicator of programming talent and creativity.

How can I help?

There are multiple ways to support our organization: you can donate money directly to HackSwift, which helps us meet all our needs; send mentors or engineers to inspire students; and provide students with essential programming tools, like hosting, hardware, and API credits, for the event.
